Yes — Japanese, Korean, German, American, and bakkies of every flavour. From a Polo to a Land Cruiser to a 7-series, our team has the gear and the know-how.

Always. WhatsApp us a description (a photo or video helps) and we'll come back with a written quote, usually the same day.
